## 📝 Risk Management

### Technical Risks
- Performance issues with scale → Mitigation: Early optimization, monitoring
- Browser compatibility → Mitigation: Cross-browser testing
- Data loss → Mitigation: Regular backups, redundancy

### Business Risks
- Low user engagement → Mitigation: User testing, iterate quickly
- Competitive pressure → Mitigation: Focus on quality and values
- Monetization challenges → Mitigation: Multiple revenue streams

### Content Risks
- Story quality issues → Mitigation: Editorial review process
- Inappropriate content → Mitigation: Content moderation, parent oversight
- Cultural sensitivity → Mitigation: Diverse review team
