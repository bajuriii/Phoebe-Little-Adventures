**Series**: Phoebe Little Adventures
**Target Age**: 3-6 years old

## 👧 Main Character: Phoebe

### Character Profile
- **Age**: 5 years old
- **Personality**: Curious, kind, brave, thoughtful
- **Appearance**: A cheerful girl with diverse representation in different versions
- **Core Values**: Kindness, honesty, courage, helping others
- **Strengths**: Problem-solving, empathy, creativity
- **Growth Areas**: Learning to overcome fears, understanding emotions

### Character Arc (Series-wide)
Phoebe grows from a curious child exploring her immediate world to a confident young adventurer who seeks to help others and make positive differences.

## 📋 Story Structure Template

### Standard Story Format

```
Title: [Story Title]
Chapter: Chapter [X]
Pages: 12-15
Themes: [Primary, Secondary]
Lessons: [Main lesson and values]

Page 1: Introduction & Setting
- Establish location and mood
- Introduce Phoebe and scenario
- Create curiosity/interest

Page 2-3: Conflict/Challenge
- Present the problem
- Show Phoebe's emotional response
- Set up the adventure

Page 4-6: Development
- Phoebe takes action
- Meets supporting characters
- Learns important lessons

Page 7-10: Climax
- Phoebe faces the main challenge
- Shows courage, kindness, or wisdom
- Turning point moment

Page 11-12: Resolution
- Problem solved with positive outcome
- Phoebe reflects on lessons
- Hopeful, warm ending
```

## ✏️ Writing Guidelines

### Language Level
- **Vocabulary**: Simple, 3-6 year-old friendly
- **Sentences**: Short, clear, easy to follow
- **Concepts**: Age-appropriate but not condescending
- **Length**: 3-5 minute read time

### Tone
- Warm and encouraging
- Positive and hopeful
- Inclusive and welcoming
- Never scary or overwhelming
- Solution-focused

### Content Requirements
- ✅ Positive role models
- ✅ Diverse characters and situations
- ✅ Clear life lessons
- ✅ Happy, satisfying endings
- ✅ Encourages reading and learning
- ✅ Age-appropriate challenges

### Content Restrictions
- ❌ No violence or scary imagery
- ❌ No sad or tragic endings
- ❌ No excessive sadness (can have moments, but always resolved)
- ❌ No complex family situations
- ❌ No inappropriate content
- ❌ No advertising or brand placement

## 🎨 Illustration Guidelines

### Visual Style
- Bright, appealing colors
- Child-friendly character designs
- Diverse representation
- Warm, inviting environments
- Expressive character emotions
- Clear, uncluttered compositions

### Character Representation
- Include diverse skin tones
- Show various family structures
- Represent different abilities
- Include diverse clothing styles
- Show children and adults together
- Feature animals as supporting characters

### Scene Design
- Outdoor settings (parks, forests, gardens)
- Cozy indoor spaces
- Nature elements (trees, flowers, sky)
- Clear focal points
- Age-appropriate scale and proportions

When creating a new story:

- [ ] Write first draft
- [ ] Age-appropriateness review
- [ ] Inclusivity check
- [ ] Emotional tone review
- [ ] Edit and refine
- [ ] Create illustrations
- [ ] Final review and approval
