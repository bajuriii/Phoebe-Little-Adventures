# Story 07 — The Lost Little Star

## Title
The Lost Little Star

## Subtitle
A bedtime story about kindness, courage, and helping others.

## Target Age
3–6 years old

## Moral
Even the smallest act of kindness can light up someone's world.

---

# Cover

Title:
The Lost Little Star

Subtitle:
A bedtime story about kindness and friendship.

---

# Page 1

One peaceful evening,
Phoebe looked up at the sparkling night sky.

Hundreds of little stars twinkled above her.

---

# Page 2

Suddenly...

One tiny star slipped from the sky.

It landed softly beside Phoebe.

---

# Page 3

The little star was crying.

"I can't find my way home."

Phoebe smiled kindly.

---

# Page 4

"Don't worry," said Phoebe.

"I'll help you."

The little star smiled again.

---

# Page 5

Together,
they walked into the magical forest.

Fireflies danced around them.

---

# Page 6

A wise old owl watched from a tree.

"I know the way," the owl said.

---

# Page 7

The owl pointed toward a glowing hill.

"Follow the light."

Phoebe thanked the owl.

---

# Page 8

Phoebe climbed the hill.

The little star began shining brighter and brighter.

---

# Page 9

At the top of the hill,

the little star slowly floated into the sky.

A beautiful beam of light surrounded it.

---

# Page 10

The little star found its family.

The whole night sky sparkled brightly.

Phoebe smiled happily.

---

# Page 11

The little star twinkled as if saying,

"Thank you, Phoebe."

Phoebe waved goodbye.

---

# Page 12

Phoebe walked home with a happy heart.

She knew that helping others made the world brighter.

The End.
