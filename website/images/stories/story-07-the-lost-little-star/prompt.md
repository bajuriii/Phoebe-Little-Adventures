\# Story 07 Illustration Prompt



\## Master Prompt



Use the attached Official Phoebe Character Sheet as the mandatory reference.



Maintain identical:



\- face

\- hairstyle

\- clothing

\- shoes

\- body proportions

\- illustration style

\- color palette



Phoebe is a cheerful 3-year-old girl.



Do NOT redesign the character.



Only change:



\- pose

\- expression

\- camera angle

\- lighting

\- environment

\- action



Illustration Style:



\- High-quality children's picture book

\- Soft pastel colors

\- Warm cinematic lighting

\- Cozy bedtime atmosphere

\- Painterly digital illustration

\- Rounded shapes

\- Rich details

\- Friendly expressions



Composition:



\- Landscape orientation

\- Aspect Ratio 4:3

\- Leave negative space for story text

\- Keep important subjects away from page edges



No text.

No watermark.

No logo.



\---



\## Page Prompts



\### Cover



Phoebe reaches toward a tiny glowing star under a magical night sky.



\---



\### Page 1



Phoebe stands in a grassy meadow at twilight.

She looks up at hundreds of sparkling stars.

The moon shines softly.

Gentle wind moves the flowers.

Phoebe smiles with wonder.

Peaceful bedtime atmosphere.



\---



\### Page 2



A tiny glowing star falls gently from the night sky.

A magical trail of sparkling stardust follows behind.

Phoebe watches with wide curious eyes.

The meadow softly glows from the star's light.



\---



\### Page 3



A tiny glowing star sits on the grass crying softly.

Phoebe kneels beside the little star with a warm caring smile.

Moonlight gently lights the scene.

The environment feels comforting and safe.



\---



\### Page 4



Phoebe gently holds the tiny star in both hands.

The little star begins smiling again.

Soft golden light surrounds them.

Tiny glowing particles float through the air.

The scene should communicate kindness and friendship.



\---



\### Page 5



Phoebe walks through a magical forest while holding the tiny glowing star.

Friendly trees.

Colorful flowers.

Fireflies.

Small mushrooms.

The forest feels magical and welcoming, never scary.



\---



\### Page 6



A friendly wise owl sits on an old tree branch.

Phoebe looks up while listening carefully.

The little glowing star floats beside her.

Moonlight shines through the forest leaves.

Warm magical atmosphere.



\---



\### Page 7



The wise owl spreads one wing toward a distant glowing hill.

Phoebe happily thanks the owl.

The little star shines brighter with hope.

A magical forest path leads into the distance.



\---



\### Page 8



Phoebe climbs a gentle grassy hill together with the little glowing star.

The night sky slowly becomes brighter.

Many stars begin appearing overhead.

Soft magical lighting.

Dreamlike atmosphere.



\---



\### Page 9



The little glowing star slowly floats upward into the night sky.

A magical beam of light connects the hill with the stars above.

Phoebe waves happily.

The sky sparkles with hundreds of tiny stars.



\---



\### Page 10



The little star rejoins the other stars.

The entire night sky glows beautifully.

Phoebe stands smiling proudly.

Rolling hills shimmer under the starlight.

Magical celebration atmosphere.



\---



\### Page 11



Phoebe walks home through the peaceful flower meadow.

One tiny star twinkles brightly above her.

The star appears to wave goodbye.

Calm bedtime atmosphere.

Soft moonlight.

Warm pastel colors.



\---



\### Page 12



Phoebe sits by her bedroom window wearing the same outfit.

She looks out at the beautiful night sky.

The little star shines brightly among hundreds of stars.

The bedroom feels warm, cozy, peaceful, and safe.

Leave empty space at the bottom for the story's moral message.
